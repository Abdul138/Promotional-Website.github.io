Liverpool

Liverpool is a top teir profesional soccer club in England. They compete in the premier league with other top teir teams. They were founded in 1892 and have played its home games at Anfield.

Liverpools History

Liverpool F.C was funded in 1892 and joined the football league in 1893. During the Tom Watson (manager) era, Liverpool had it's first successful era which they had won two championships. However they would be relegated in later years to the second division.
In 1959 Bill Shankly was appointed as manager and would be later known as Liverpool's greatest manager. He helped the club back into the first division and they won multiple league championships and four European Cups.. In 1985 there was a disaster in the Heysel Stadium which all English clubs were banned from European competitions until 1991. However they were still able to win three more league titles in this period.
